Final Fantasy XIII-2 Clock Puzzle Solver

Objective:

The goal of the puzzle is to travel from circle to circle around the clock while only landing on each circle exactly once. You can start the puzzle from any circle on the clock face. The number that is inscribed on the current circle describes the number of moves in the CW or CCW direction to the next circle that you can move to. If there are no more circles you can legally travel to and the puzzle is still not solved then you need to start over.

Directions:

Insert the numbers clockwise from the 12 o'Clock position for the puzzle you are trying to solve. Once all numbers have been entered, press the 'Solve' button to display the solutions to that puzzle.
